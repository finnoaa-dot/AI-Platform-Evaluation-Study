# AI Platform Evaluation and Usability Mixed-Methods Study

A comprehensive 3-phase mixed-methods research program examining TELUS Digital's internally built AI platform across the full employee base to quantify the business value and user experience impact of AI copilots in daily task completion.

## Executive Summary

This study evaluated the adoption, usability, and business impact of AI copilots integrated into TELUS Digital's internal platform. The findings reveal significant productivity gains and improvements in employee well-being:

- **108 hours saved per employee annually**
- **$7M+ in annual cost savings for the firm**
- **70% average time savings on tasks** for copilot users
- **Improved work/life balance** and reduced context switching
- **Enhanced creative capacity** for iterative work and new idea development

## Key Findings

### Productivity Impact
Employees who use AI copilots save an average of **70% time on task**, enabling them to focus on higher-value creative work and problem-solving.

### Employee Well-being
Beyond productivity metrics, copilot users report:
- Better work/life balance
- Decreased stress from repetitive tasks
- Reduced context switching between applications
- More time for strategic thinking and innovation

### Business Value
The quantifiable business impact demonstrates clear ROI:
- **Per-employee savings**: 108 hours annually
- **Firm-wide savings**: $7M+ annually
- Framework developed for measuring and scaling copilot benefits across the organization

## Research Methodology

This 3-phase mixed-methods research program combined qualitative and quantitative approaches:

### Phase 1: Discovery & Baseline
- Employee interviews and focus groups
- Platform usage analysis
- Workflow mapping

### Phase 2: Evaluation & Testing
- Usability testing sessions
- A/B testing scenarios
- Comparative workflow analysis

### Phase 3: Impact Measurement & Framework Development
- Quantitative benefit analysis
- Framework creation for copilot value measurement
- Recommendations for scaling adoption

## Impact & Applications

This research created a replicable framework for organizations evaluating AI tool adoption. The findings demonstrate that properly implemented AI copilots don't just increase productivity—they enhance employee satisfaction and enable teams to focus on strategic, creative work.

### Key Insights for Organizations
- AI tool adoption requires comprehensive evaluation beyond basic usage metrics
- Employee well-being and productivity gains are interconnected
- Quantifiable frameworks are essential for scaling AI initiatives
- Change management and training are critical success factors
